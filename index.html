Discover Digha, West Bengal

Digha is a popular seaside resort town in the Purba Medinipur district of West Bengal, India. It is known for its beautiful beaches, serene atmosphere, and stunning sunsets. A perfect getaway for families and friends, Digha offers a blend of natural beauty and vibrant local culture. Originally known as Beerkul, Digha was discovered by the British in the late 18th century. Today, it stands as one of the most beloved beach destinations in Eastern India.

Explore Digha's Enchanting Locations

Old Digha

The original charm of Digha, famous for its rip-rap stone structures, casuarina plantations, and the Subarnarekha River estuary.

Tourist Attractions:

  • Mohona (Estuary): A picturesque spot where the river meets the sea.
  • Marine Aquarium and Regional Centre (MARC): Home to diverse marine life.
  • Digha Gate: An iconic entrance landmark.

New Digha

A more planned and modern extension of Digha, offering a cleaner beach and more developed tourist facilities.

Famous Attractions:

  • New Digha Beach: Wider and less crowded beach ideal for relaxing.
  • Digha Science Centre & National Science Camp: Interactive exhibits and a planetarium.
  • Amarabati Park: A beautiful park with boating facilities.

Dheusagar Park

A new and popular amusement park in Digha, offering a serene escape with various recreational facilities and a tranquil environment near the sea.

Attractions:

  • Amusement Rides: Features a variety of rides for all ages.
  • Boating Facilities: Enjoy serene boat rides on the park's lake.
  • Toy Train: A delightful ride, especially for children.
  • Lush Greenery and Gardens: Beautifully maintained landscapes for relaxation and picnics.

Udaipur Beach

Located on the border of West Bengal and Odisha, Udaipur Beach is known for its pristine beauty and casuarina trees.

Attraction:

  • Pristine Beach and Casuarina Groves: Ideal for peaceful walks and picnics.

Talsari Beach

A serene beach located in Odisha, just a short drive from Digha, famous for its red crabs and palm trees.

Attraction:

  • Red Crabs and Palm Fringed Beach: A unique natural spectacle.

Digha Mohona

The confluence point of the Champa River and the Bay of Bengal, offering a vibrant fish market and scenic views.

Attraction:

  • Fish Market and Estuary View: Experience local life and enjoy fresh seafood.

Tajpur

An emerging beach destination known for its tranquility, dense casuarina groves, and thousands of red crabs.

Attraction:

  • Red Crab Beach and Serene Environment: Perfect for a quiet retreat.

Digha Jagannath Mandir

A newly built replica of the Puri Jagannath Temple, offering spiritual solace and architectural beauty.

Attraction:

  • Spiritual Shrine and Architecture: A significant religious landmark.

Amaravati Park

A well-maintained park in New Digha with a toy train, boating facilities, and lush greenery, popular among families.

Attraction:

  • Boating, Toy Train, and Greenery: Family-friendly entertainment.

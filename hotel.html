Top Attractions in Digha

New Digha Beach

New Digha, Digha, West Bengal 721463

New Digha Beach is a newly developed beach known for its clean sand, calm waves, and peaceful atmosphere. It is ideal for people who are looking for a quiet walk along the shore while enjoying the gentle breeze. It also offers beautiful sunrise/sunset views and local seafood.

Old Digha Beach

Old Digha, Digha, West Bengal 721428

Old Digha Beach offers a serene retreat with distinctive stone formations and relatively less tourism compared to New Digha. It's a picturesque haven for tranquility and natural beauty.

Marine Aquarium and Regional Centre (MARC)

State Highway 57, Digha, West Bengal 721428

The Marine Aquarium and Regional Centre (MARC) showcases a diverse range of marine life, from 'local and curious' species to freshwater creatures, providing visitors with a unique opportunity to marvel at sea anemones, lobsters, sharks, rays, butterfly fishes, sea snakes, and seahorses. It also has research and museum wings.

Amrabati Park

New Digha, Digha, West Bengal 721463

Amrabati Park is a delightful oasis with immaculate lawns, well-paved walkways, and a crystal clear lake. Visitors can enjoy boating, a ropeway ride, and explore the natural flora and fauna. It's a perfect spot for picnics and relaxation.

Digha Science Centre

Fore Shore Road, Digha, West Bengal 721428

The Digha Science Centre & National Science Camp is a remarkable destination that marries education with entertainment. It features interactive exhibits on fun and life science, reflections, a 3D theatre, Jurassic Park with light and sound, and a Science Park, providing a unique experience for all ages.

Digha Mohona (Watch Point)

Mohana, Digha, West Bengal 721428

Digha Mohona Watch Point offers breathtaking panoramic views where the Champa River meets the Bay of Bengal. It's a captivating spot for photography, leisurely walks, enjoying spectacular sunsets, and experiencing a serene coastal ambiance.

Talsari Beach (Near Digha)

Talsari, Odisha (approximately 10-15 km from Digha)

Talsari Beach, located near Digha on the Odisha-West Bengal border, is a serene and untouched beach known for its red crabs, scenic shores, and peaceful ambiance. It offers a quieter retreat for those looking to escape crowds and enjoy nature.

Chandaneswar Temple (Near Digha)

Chandaneswar, Odisha (approximately 6-8 km from Digha)

Chandaneswar Temple is a famous Shiva temple located in Chandaneswar, Odisha. It is known for its grand architecture and is a revered pilgrimage site, especially during the Chadak Mela and Maha Shivaratri Festival, attracting lakhs of devotees.

Udaypur Sea Beach

JF6M+CRW, Udaypur, Odisha 721428

Udaypur Sea Beach, located near the border of Odisha and West Bengal, is a tranquil and less crowded beach known for its pristine natural beauty. It offers a peaceful escape with casuarina trees lining the shore, ideal for relaxation and enjoying the serene coastal environment.

Shree Shree Jagannath Dev Mandir Digha

VILL: KHADALGOBRA, P. O: DIGHA, P. S: DIGHA, EAST MEDINIPUR EAST, Gadadharpur, Digha, West Bengal 721428

Shree Shree Jagannath Dev Mandir Digha is a revered temple in Digha, dedicated to Lord Jagannath. It's known for its beautiful architecture and religious significance, attracting devotees and tourists alike.
